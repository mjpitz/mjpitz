I'm a software engineer with over 20 years of experience building systems that scale. I specialize in **DevOps, observability, and infrastructure** - helping organizations build reliable, maintainable platforms. Currently, I offer [technical consulting](/collab) for individuals advancing their careers and companies optimizing their tech stack.

I've been programming since I was a kid. At 15, I started my first website development company. At 22, I co-founded a startup building tools for job seekers. Since then, I've worked across diverse organizations - from startups to enterprises - solving complex technical challenges and leading engineering teams.

I believe in **building in public** and giving back to the community. You'll find my work on [GitHub](https://github.com/mjpitz), my thoughts in [blog posts](/blog), and my [Helm charts](/charts) that others can use.

#### Beyond the keyboard

Outside of work, I'm an avid [hockey][] player and intermediate pianist (you can find my recordings in the [media section](/media)). I'm also a parent to both humans and pets, and I volunteer for a state-wide search and rescue organization. 

Something you may notice: this site uses a hyperlegible font. I often struggle to interpret characters when reading on screens, so I favor screen readers for consuming content - including my own writing.

#### What I'm working on

Currently, I'm a lead IC at [gigawatt.ai](https://gigawatt.ai) where I've been for the past 6+ months. In parallel, I'm doing sporadic work on **C8 Labs**, reworking the operating model and exploring new directions.

These days, I'm deeply into the intersection of **software, hardware, and 3D printing** - bringing together my technical skills with hands-on fabrication. When I have time, I still tinker with [Raspberry Pi][] clusters.
