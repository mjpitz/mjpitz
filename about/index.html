Our Story

Inspired by our founders direct experience volunteering with a state-wide search and rescue organization in Texas, C8 Labs aims to bring real-time, AI driven insights and decision making to the very edge. First responders often find themselves working in network congested or isolated environments, making cloud-based AI solutions unreliable at best.

While we are developing a cloud-hosted solution as a near-term solution that addresses several needs from responders, we recognize that access to the internet is a privilege. Through close collaboration with various organizations, we are working to determine the offline needs of responders out in the field.
